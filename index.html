My Top 5 Movies 2025

1. K-pop Demon Hunters

Netflix • ⭐ 5/5

Great plot and character design. I'm obsessed with the main song "Golden"; it's a masterpiece!

2. Pearl

Netflix • ⭐ 5/5

A tragic yet brilliant story. Mia Goth delivers one of the best performances of the decade. Simply haunting.

3. The Fantastic Four

Disney+ • ⭐ 5/5

A breath of fresh air for the MCU. Incredible effects and a solid villain in Galactus, the Devourer of Worlds.

4. Avatar: Fire and Ashes

In Theaters • ⭐ 4/5

Stunning visuals, though the plot feels familiar. It's a bit long (3+ hours!), but it definitely doesn't disappoint.

5. Weapons

Max • ⭐ 3/5

Told in a very novel and intriguing way. You must watch until the end, even if the finale feels a bit short.
