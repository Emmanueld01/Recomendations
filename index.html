My Top 5 Movies 2025

1. K-pop Demon Hunters

Great plot and character design. I'm obsessed with the main song "Golden"; it's a masterpiece!

2. Pearl

Netflix • ⭐ 5/5

A tragic story but with a great plot. The lead actress is literally one of the best of the decade; her acting in this movie is just insane!

3. The Fantastic Four

Disney+ • ⭐ 5/5

This is finally a breath of fresh air for Marvel. The effects and the story are great, and Galactus is such a solid villain!

4. Avatar: Fire and Ashes

In Theaters • ⭐ 4/5

The effects are crazy good, but the plot is very similar to the second one. Plus, 3 hours is way too long for me! But overall, it's still a good watch.

5. Weapons

Max • ⭐ 3/5

The way the story is told is super new and intriguing. You really have to watch until the very end to get it, though I feel the ending was a bit "meh" compared to the rest of the movie.
